Need help with LicMan?

LicMan is AppFire LLC. budget planning and expense tracking tool.

In AppFire we are committed to ensuring we spend only on meaningful value generating activities.

Usually we carefully plan the Planned budget for the next fiscal year and it goes through approval.

When the budget is approved we start placing purchase orders in Coupa (our Procurement system) either as Contracts or Virtual Payment Cards.

The Expense is the most important part of the system. It is the planned expense which has its budget approved.

An Expense has many attributes such as:

  • Vendor
  • Product
  • Category
  • Organization
  • Group
  • Cost Per Unit
  • Number of Units
  • Approved Budget
  • Contracted Value
  • Sunset flag
  • Sunset plan
  • Notes
  • as well as specific attributes based on the selected Category.
